How to Use AirPiano

AirPiano is a revolutionary way to create music using just your hand gestures. Here's how to get started:

  1. Click the Start AirPiano button to begin hand tracking.
  2. Position your hands in view of the camera.
  3. Raise individual fingers to play single chords:
    • Thumb for D Major (left) or A Major (right)
    • Index for E Minor (left) or B Minor (right)
    • And more as shown in the Chord Reference panel
  4. Combine fingers for special chord combinations.
  5. Lower your fingers to stop playing the chord (with sustain).

The piano visualization at the bottom shows which keys are being played, with different colors for left and right hand control.

Adjust sustain time, sensitivity, and instrument selection in the Settings panel to customize your experience.

About AirPiano

AirPiano is a cutting-edge musical interface that transforms hand gestures into beautiful chord progressions using computer vision technology.

This project combines advanced hand tracking with MIDI sound synthesis to create an intuitive and engaging musical experience without requiring any physical instruments.

Technologies used:

  • Hand tracking with OpenCV and MediaPipe
  • Real-time sound generation with PyGame MIDI
  • Web interface with Flask and Socket.IO
  • 3D visualizations with Three.js

This project was developed as a modern approach to music creation, making it accessible to everyone regardless of musical training.
